About Us

Welcome to Jassi TaxI Service

Jassi Travel is a Chandigarh based Taxi Service. Operating from Chandigarh, Jassi Taxi Service has been offering tour and travel services to individual tourists and travel enthusiasts and for corporates too. JassiTaxi was set up by Mr. Jassi Singh in 2020. Today the fleet of cars available with Jassi Taxi Service ranges from sedans, hatchbacks, minivans, crossovers, Tempo travelers, to SUVs, luxury cars and 4x4 off road vehicles too.

Jassi Taxi Service takes utmost care to ensure every ride is COVID-19 compliant. We sanitize and fumigate our vehicles properly. The drivers undertake regular COVID-tests and wear gloves and masks all the time.
